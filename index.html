Our Story

Most of us are tired in life. There! We said it!
We want to be billionaires. We want to chill on the beach. We want to make a movie. We tolerate our bosses. We despise our schedules. We hate our jobs and want to look for new ones!

The Bah is a place that lets you pour out all your troubles.
It's a casual, open, welcoming, non pretentious bar that allows you to have a chilled drink with friends or enjoy a book & a beer by yourself. It allows you to be who you are, indulge in some wholesome soul food, awesome drinks & efficient yet friendly service. That's THE BAH for you!

A pun on the word bar. An interjection that encapsulates all that we want to express about our day. A safe haven from the sting of daily mundane.

Our Menu

Our menu celebrates the wholesome goodness of a mix of cuisine, with a smattering of the local flavour. Our menu is a tribute to the fresh ingredients, in house-handmade breads, pastas and sauces, with hearty flavors, and time-honored recipes with a modern twist.
From farm-fresh salads bursting with vibrant colors to comforting soups that warm the soul, to burgers that leave you drooling, steaks that are done perfect, desserts that remind you of grandma's love and han churned ice creams with never before flavours - each dish on our menu is crafted with care to nourish both body and spirit. We believe that good food is the foundation of good moods, and we invite you to join us in savoring the simple pleasures of wholesome fare.
