Our Story

Offering the city's finest craft brews, in a minimalistic ambience, blending wood, cement, brick, and steel, it's the ideal spot to unwind after a long day. The outdoor seating, open to the sky and overlooking the Kadugodi forest, enhances the relaxing experience, while the large bar inside serves as the focal point of the space.

Why we are called “The Bah”
Most of us are tired in life. There! We said it! We want to be billionaires. We want to chill on the beach. We want to make a movie. We tolerate our bosses. We despise our schedules. We hate our jobs and want to look for new ones!

The Bah is a place that lets you pour out all your troubles. It's a casual, open, welcoming, non pretentious bar that allows you to have a chilled drink with friends or enjoy a book & a beer by yourself. It allows you to be who you are, indulge in some wholesome soul food, awesome drinks & efficient yet friendly service. That's THE BAH for you!

A pun on the word bar. An interjection that encapsulates all that we want to express about our day. A safe haven from the sting of daily mundane.
